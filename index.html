introduction chess

Chess is a two-player strategic board game played on an 8x8 grid called a chessboard. Each player controls 16 pieces: king, queen, rooks, bishops, knights, and pawns. The goal is to checkmate your opponent’s king—trapping it so it cannot escape capture. Chess combines deep strategy, tactics, and skill. It’s called the “game of kings” and has been played worldwide for centuries.
